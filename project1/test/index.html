ABOUT US
Our philosophy in designing Play On Fitness has been to create an environment that offers a sanctuary from the stresses and strains of modern life.

Affordable membership and a humble community atmosphere means this Bangalore based fitness club attracts a healthy mix of people from well-honed men bulking up to more average-shaped folk who just wants to keep fit. There's an open gym and exercise studio which make up the perfect gym facility. The cardio weights and machines are all of International Standards by Edge Fitness which are world famous for their ergonomics.


" When you finish a good workout,
    you feel great about yourself. "

We believe that all our members will appreciate the care and attention that has been paid to every detail of the club. The quality of the facilities, high standards of cleanliness and customer service which we promise will be reflected in an atmosphere rarely enjoyed in other clubs.
